Bakery beginners course

Course content :

This preliminary course is focus on give to our students the basic knowledge of bakery preparation with theorical and pratical application creating a solid background with which in the end of the course they can start their carrer as Baker assistant.


Some main themes addressed in this course are :

  • Scientific reasons behind the creation of gluten network
  • Importance of bread in the world
  • Culture and tradition of bread usage
  • Bread in History

    • Completing this course give the possibility to the learner to have a priority access to the Advanced Bakery course.


    Requirements:

    The only requirements request for this level is be over 18 years old and have the wish to start a new carrer pathway and learn about this profession.


    Duration:

    3 weeks


    Cost :

    1000 €

    Advanced Bakery course

    Course content :

    This advanced course is focus on the study of the different flour and all the possible application during multiple kind of preparation and modern techniques of sweet and salted panification giving the knowledge to enter in the labour market with a qualification of Baker chef.


    Some main themes addressed in this course are :

    • Focus on ancient grains, properties and the possible applications in the modern panification
    • Croissant, story and preparation and manual puff pastry
    • Different kind of lievitations and which preparation suits more
    • Gluten-free world and how deal with it

    In this course is included bymontly the intervention of host bakers that bring everytime new knowledge to enrich your range of skills and pratical application in daily basis working issues.

    Requirements:

    Required at least an hotel school diploma or 6 month of working experience

    Succesfully pass our entrance test with a minimum vote of 60/100


    Duration :

    30 weeks


    Cost :

    10000 €
