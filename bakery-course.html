Bakery beginners course

Course content :

This preliminary course has the focus to deliver our students the basic knowledge of bakery preparation with theorical and pratical applications, creating a solid background in order for them to start their carrers as Assistant baker chefs, at the end of the course.


Some main themes addressed in this course are :

  • Scientific reasons behind the creation of gluten network
  • Importance of bread in the world
  • Culture and tradition of bread usage
  • Bread in History

    • Completing this course learners will have a priority access to the Advanced Bakery course.


    Requirements:

    The only requirements requested for this level is being over 18 years old and wish to start a new carrer pathway and learn about this profession.


    Duration:

    3 weeks


    Cost :

    1000 €

    Advanced Bakery course

    Course content :

    This advanced course will be focused on the study of the different flours and all their possible applications during multiple kind of preparations and modern techniques of sweet and savoury panification, delivering the necessary knowledge to enter in the labour market with a of Baker chef qualification.


    Some main themes addressed in this course are :

    • Focus on ancient grains, properties and the possible applications in the modern panification
    • Croissant, story and preparation and manual puff pastry
    • Different kind of lievitations and which preparation suits more
    • Gluten-free world and how to deal with it

    This course includes bymontly intervention of host bakers that will always bring new knowledge to the table, to enrich your range of skills and pratical application in daily basis working issues.

    Requirements:

    An hotel school diploma or 6 months of working experience are essential

    Succesfully pass our entrance test with a minimum vote of 60/100


    Duration :

    30 weeks


    Cost :

    10000 €
