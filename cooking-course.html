Cooking beginners course

Course content :

This preliminary course is focus on give to our students the basic knowledge of cooking preparation with theorical and pratical application creating a solid background with which in the end of the course they can start their carrer as Commis chef.


Some main themes addressed in this course are :

  • Different kind of knife
  • Ingredients and how use it at their best
  • Importance of cultural differences in the food
  • Cooking procedures and history

    • Completing this course give the possibility to the learner to have a priority access to the Advanced Cooking course.


    Requirements:

    The only requirements request for this level is be over 18 years old and have the wish to start a new carrer pathway and learn about this profession.


    Duration:

    3 weeks


    Cost :

    1800 €


    Advanced Cooking course

    Course content :

    This advanced course is focus on teach the right butchery procedures, cooking preparations and usage of fat, molecular kitchen and avant-garde techniques of nouvelle cuisine.


    Some main themes addressed in this course are :

    • Science and cuisine, a marriage that have changed the culinary world
    • Importance of choose the right fat for every cooking procedure
    • Sous-vide and all his applications
    • Vegan and the different kind of diet

    In this course is included bymontly the intervention of host chefs that bring everytime new knowledge to enrich your range of skills and pratical application in daily basis working issues.


    Requirements:

    Required at least an hotel school diploma or 6 month of working experience

    Succesfully pass our entrance test with a minimum vote of 60/100


    Duration :

    30 weeks


    Cost :

    18000 €
