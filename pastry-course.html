Pastry beginners course

Course content :

This preliminary course is focus on give to our students the basic knowledge of pastry preparation with theorical and pratical application creating a solid background with which in the end of the course they can start their carrer as Pastry chef assistant.


Some main themes addressed in this course are :

  • Air and his importance in pastry preparation
  • Chocolate, his properties, composition and usage in a recipe
  • Sauces and glazing
  • Pastry in history

Completing this course give the possibility to the learner to have a priority access to the Advanced Pastry course.


Requirements :

The only requirements request for this level is be over 18 years old and have the wish to start a new carrer pathway and learn about this profession.


Duration :

3 weeks

Cost :

1500 €

Advanced Pastry course

Course content :

This advanced course is centered in the learning of the most excellent techniques of pastry used in Gourmet restaurant and Pastry laboratory giving you the possibility to enter in the labour market with a level of skills enough to be responible of any kind of preparation.


Some main themes addressed in this course are :

  • Chocolate tempering
  • Ice cream preparation and fundamentals to balancing the formula for the perfect consistency
  • Leavened cakes and Mother Yeast
  • Sugar Sculture and malleability of this essential ingredient

In this course is included bymontly the intervention of host pastry chefs that bring everytime new knowledge to enrich your range of skills and pratical application in daily basis working issues.

Requirements :

Required at least an hotel school diploma or 6 month of working experience

Succesfully pass our entrance test with a minimum vote of 60/100


Duration :

30 weeks

Cost :

15000 €
